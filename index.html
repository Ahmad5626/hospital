Comprehensive Cancer Care Services

Chemotherapy

Chemotherapy uses medications to target and destroy cancer cells. Dr. Babu creates personalized treatment plans to minimize side effects. His expertise ensures optimal outcomes.

Learn More

Immunotherapy

Immunotherapy boosts the immune system to fight cancer. Dr. Babu utilizes innovative approaches to stimulate immune response. This treatment enhances cancer-fighting capabilities.

Learn More

Bone Marrow Transplantation

Bone Marrow Transplantation replaces damaged marrow with healthy cells. Dr. Babu performs autologous and allogenic transplants for blood disorders. His expertise ensures successful transplants.

Learn More

CAR-T Cell Therapy

CAR-T Cell Therapy uses genetically modified cells to target cancer. Dr. Babu offers advanced treatment for leukemia, lymphoma, and myeloma. This therapy provides hope for complex cases.
