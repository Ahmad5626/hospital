Our Services

Comprehensive Cancer Care Services

Chemotherapy

Chemotherapy uses medications to target and destroy cancer cells. Dr. G. Vasu Babu creates personalized treatment plans to minimize side effects. His expertise ensures optimal outcomes.

Immunotherapy

Immunotherapy boosts the immune system to fight cancer. Dr. G. Vasu Babu utilizes innovative approaches to stimulate immune response. This treatment enhances cancer-fighting capabilities.

Bone Marrow Transplantation

Bone Marrow Transplantation replaces damaged marrow with healthy cells. Dr. G. Vasu Babu performs autologous and allogenic transplants for blood disorders. His expertise ensures successful transplants.

CAR-T Cell Therapy

CAR-T Cell Therapy uses genetically modified cells to target cancer. Dr. G. Vasu Babu offers advanced treatment for leukemia, lymphoma, and myeloma. This therapy provides hope for complex cases.

Leukaemia Treatment

Dr. G. Vasu Babu manages acute and chronic leukemia with expert care. His treatment plans incorporate latest research and technologies. Patients receive comprehensive support.

Lymphoma Treatment

Dr. G. Vasu Babu treats Hodgkin and non-Hodgkin lymphoma with personalized approaches. His expertise combines medication, radiation, and immunotherapy. Patients receive optimal care.

Myeloma Treatment

Dr. G. Vasu Babu offers advanced therapies for multiple myeloma. His treatment plans focus on symptom management and disease control. Patients receive compassionate care.

Blood Disorders Management

Dr. G. Vasu Babu manages anemia, thrombocytopenia, and bleeding disorders. His expertise ensures effective treatment and symptom management. Patients receive comprehensive care.
