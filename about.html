Dr. G. Vasu Babu is a highly qualified and experienced medical professional specializing in Hematology, Oncology, and Bone Marrow Transplantation. He holds a DNB (Medicine), DM in Medical Oncology from Tata Memorial Hospital (TMH) Mumbai, and a Fellowship in Bone Marrow Transplantation from Jaslok Hospital, Mumbai. With this extensive training and dedication to excellence, Dr. G. Vasu Babu brings valuable knowledge and expertise to Yashoda Hospitals, where he continues to make significant contributions to patient care.

Dr. G. Vasu Babu’s career is distinguished by his previous role as an Assistant Professor at Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai, where he gained substantial experience treating complex cases of blood cancers and conducting advanced bone marrow transplantation procedures. His areas of expertise include blood cancers such as Leukemia, Lymphoma, and Myeloma, along with various solid tumors including Breast Cancer, Gynecologic Cancers, Thyroid Cancer, Head and Neck Cancers, and Gastric Cancer. In addition to his proficiency in Chemotherapy, Immunotherapy, and Precision Targeted Therapy, he is one of the few specialists trained in CAR-T Cell Therapy, a cutting-edge treatment option for certain cancers.

Dr. G. Vasu Babu is passionately dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-centered care. Alongside his medical treatments, he offers supportive care services, including Pain Management, Nutritional Counseling, and Psychological Support, ensuring that his patients receive holistic, comprehensive care. His commitment to staying at the forefront of oncology drives him to actively participate in research, attend international conferences, and collaborate with other experts, allowing him to offer his patients the latest and most effective treatment options.

Journey of Excellence: Dr. G. Vasu Babu’s journey in medicine began with his MBBS from N.R.I Medical College in Guntur. His desire to specialize further led him to pursue his DNB in Internal Medicine at Tata Main Hospital, Jamshedpur. He honed his skills in Medical Oncology at the esteemed Tata Memorial Center in Mumbai. He then completed a Fellowship in Bone Marrow Transplantation at Jaslok Hospital, Mumbai. His unwavering dedication to his field, combined with years of rigorous training, has positioned him as one of the leading experts in Hemato-Oncology and Bone Marrow Transplantation, continually striving to improve his patients' quality of life and outcomes.
