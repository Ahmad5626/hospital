Dr. G. Vasu Babu is a highly qualified and experienced medical professional specializing in Hematology, Oncology, and Bone Marrow Transplantation. He holds a DNB (Medicine), DM Medical Oncology (TMH Mumbai), and a Fellowship in Bone Marrow Transplantation.

Dr. G. VAsu Babu brings knowledge and expertise to Yashoda Hospitals, having previously served as an Assistant Professor at Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai. Throughout his career, he has successfully treated numerous patients with various blood cancers and provided them with advanced bone marrow transplantation procedures.

Dedicated to Patient Care11: Dr. G. VAsu Babu is passionate about providing compassionate and comprehensive care to his patients. He stays at the forefront of his field by actively participating in research and attending international conferences.

Here's a small excerpt about Dr. G. VAsu Babu's journey: Dr. G. VAsu Babu's journey began with his MBBS degree from N.R.I Medical College Guntur. He then pursued his DNB in Internal Medicine at Tata Main Hospital, Jamshedpur, followed by a Fellowship in Bone Marrow Transplantation at Jaslok Hospital Mumbai. His unwavering dedication to his field led him to pursue further training in Medical Oncology at the prestigious Tata Memorial Center/Mumbai. This steadfast commitment to learning and refining his skills has positioned him as a leading expert in his field.
